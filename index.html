Terra Prima

Terra Prima is a science fiction comic that follows the journey of an idealistic scientist, a self-absorbed collector of emotions, and a mysterious madman, as well as the people they meet along the way, as they deal with their personal challenges amidst a multiverse-wide conflict.

This story was inspired by a game my friends and I created. I started drawing it as a comic around 2010, as a practice comic since I had never drawn a long comic before, and the plan was to draw only Book One. As my drawing skills improved, I decided to continue the comic.

NOTE: since the page is still in test mode, I have only uploaded chapter 1 so far. You can read the rest of the comic on Tapas.
